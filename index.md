# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-12-23

VO2Max Timer ("the App") provides interval training timers and optional in-app purchases. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

## Data We Collect
The App itself does not require account creation and does not store personal data on our servers. Third-party services used by the App may collect limited data, including:

- Device identifiers (for advertising and fraud prevention).
- Approximate location (for ad targeting, if enabled by the ad network).
- App usage data (events such as ad impressions and app opens).
- Purchase data (for in-app purchase verification).

## How We Use Data
We use data to:
- Serve ads in the free version.
- Process and restore in-app purchases.
- Improve app performance and stability.

## Third-Party Services
The App uses the following services that may collect data:
- Google AdMob (advertising)
- Apple StoreKit (in-app purchases)

Their data practices are governed by their own privacy policies.

## Your Choices
- You can disable personalized ads by adjusting your device ad settings.
- You can opt out of tracking on iOS when prompted.
